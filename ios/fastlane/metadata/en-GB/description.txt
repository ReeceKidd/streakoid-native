Streakoid lets you define daily tasks. Which you try to complete for as many days as a row as possible.

The almighty Oid will watch over. Sending you reminders and making sure you're making time for youreself.

You can complete streaks by yourself.

Compete challenge streaks with other users around the globe.

Or you can complete streaks as a team. With a team streak if one of you doesn't do your daily task you all lose your streak. Don't let your team down.

You can view global leaderboards to see how you stack up against the rest of the world.
